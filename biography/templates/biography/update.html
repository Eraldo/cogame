Here are some questions to help you get started:
  • In what ways was your life remarkable/despicable/admirable?
  • What qualities were most influential in shaping the way you live and influence your surroundings?
  • Which quality or trait proved most troubling/difficult/beneficial?
  • Did you make any major mistakes or bad decisions? If so, what were they and how would you choose and act differently if you could do so now?
  • What are the two or three most important lessons you can pass on to your fellow legends?
  • Some people say you can judge the quality of a person's life by the enemies they make. Do you think this is true for you?
  • Was there a person in your life that guided or encouraged you in a special way?
  • Many people act out of a "code" or a set of beliefs which dictate choices (religion, politics, personal philosophy). To what extent did you act by a code or act independently of any set of beliefs? Were there times when the code was challenged and impossible to follow?
  • What do you think it means to be a hero? Have you been a hero so far?
