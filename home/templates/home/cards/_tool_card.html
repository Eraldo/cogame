Tornado Chart

When being faced with a difficult decision there are several tools that we can use to reach a good decision and maximum value potential by making ourselves conscious about what relevant and reliable information we need and what uncertainties we face. A Tornado Chart compares the relative importance of uncertainties. For each uncertainty considered there needs to be an estimate concerning possible low, base, and high outcomes.
