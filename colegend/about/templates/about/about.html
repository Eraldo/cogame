CoLegend... How it Works

The first few steps within CoLegend are common to everyone who applies but it quickly becomes customized to your specific dreams and life goals. Here's a quick glance at those initial milestones. If you have additional questions have a look at our FAQ.


APPLY FOR YOUR JOURNEY

The application process is fun, fast and free. You provide a few details about yourself, your interests and goals. Then your application is matched with a set of Mentors who can best help you achieve your goals.
Start Your Journey Now!

JOIN THE ACADEMY

CoLegend houses a rapidly growing collection of amazing development resources, including incredible tutorials and stories from amazing Legends.
Learn more about the Academy

CHOOSE A MENTOR

Our system takes all of the information from your application and uses it to find the Mentors who best suit your preference. Review them all and choose the best fit for YOU.
Start Your Journey Now!

BECOME A LEGEND

Your personalized development experience is about to begin. This is an exciting time for you and for your Mentor! Things will move faster now, so be ready!
View the Expectations of a Legend

YOUR FIRST QUEST

You'll soon receive your first Quest from your Mentor. The early Chapters help you to become familiar with the mysterious ways of CoLegend. To see an example of what's in store, have a look at the Personal Development Chapter Guide.
View an example of a Legend Path

MEET YOUR TRIBE

Each Tribe typically has about 16 Legends. We're confident you'll learn as much, if not more, from the other Legends as you will from your Mentor. They will provide you with valuable feedback, accountability and encouragement in every Chapter.
Read Stories from our Legends

DEFINE YOUR LEGEND PURPOSE

CoLegend places YOU at the center of the development experience. Your Mentor will help you to define your personal vision and goals and will then customize the Chapters to help you accomplish your main Quest. This is a key difference from traditional, content-centric development models.
Learn more about the Development Experience

DEVELOP AT YOUR OWN PACE

The pace of your progress is completely within your control. This is not a one-size fit, pass or fail, development model. We understand that each person's schedule and development pace is unique. Your Tribe will have Legends at many different Chapters.
View an example of a Development Path
