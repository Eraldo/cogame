Structure & Content

How does it work and when do we meet

For 9 months - starting in January - we meet once a month for a weekend in a group of up to 16 participants and 2 to 4 process leaders.

We will systematically build up our competencies and skills to live a fulfilled life, conscious of our needs and passions and connected with like minded people around us.

7 Life Areas build the integral framework and topic agenda over the full period of 9 months: Health, joy, power, connection, expression, mind and spirit. For each one you will learn about matching concepts and methods, get input from experts and learn how to apply them practically in your real life.

General meeting structure

The monthly meetings officially begin Saturdays at 10-18. “Officially”, meaning that people who want to meet earlier than that to use their early morning power can do so and take part in some morning fitness or meditation - depending on their likings. This is the same for Sundays, “officially” going from 11-15 o’clock. Additionally, Saturday evenings we can go have a joined dinner and enjoy a casual open-ending.

Additionally, in between those intensive weekends you will have an app to guide your ongoing personal development process. That also means having committed sharing partners with whom you can meet regularly virtually outside of the monthly meetings to reflect on your ongoing journey together.

What is the Community and for whom is it?

The quality of your journey is not only determined by where you go and how you get there. The people you travel with can make all the difference.

On your Personal Development Journey you are in good company: Committed fellow legends that you pair up with in a close duo, a supporting clan and a connected tribe - your legend family that you can open up to and get inspired by.

Lead by your Mentors you can interact regularly in a structured way so that each of you can profit the most of this shared experience. Together you can merge your potentials into an ongoing legend!

    The coAcademy is for people who want to…
  • Lead a more balanced life, listening to their mind, heart and body
  • Challenge themselves to really turn their life around and have fun while doing it
  • Have a better overview of inter- and intrapersonal processes as well as group dynamics
  • Have a deeper connection to themselves and others
  • Truly get to know and express what they’re feeling and thinking
  • Learn about different methods and exercises of personal development
  • Develop themselves, discover their real purpose in life and find out what it is they’re truly capable of
