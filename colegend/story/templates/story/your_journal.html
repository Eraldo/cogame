She slowly closed the book and shut her eyes for a moment, reflecting on what she had just read. She just had had access to an old journal entry of a fellow legend – that part was obvious. She felt a smile stretch across her face as she went through some of the passages in her head again – she had had quite similar thoughts after having joined coLegend. It felt good to know that there were others feeling the same way. She suddenly felt the urge to start writing herself – she wanted to write down this experience, her finding this marvellous tree, her thoughts about what she wanted out of the platform…

A woodpecker suddenly disturbed the silence, pecking at the tree. She tried to ignore the disturbing sound as she still wanted to continue her train of thoughts for a bit. But when a sunbeam suddenly touched her face she opened her eyes, surprised. The woodpecker had made a whole into the bark and the ray of sunshine was directly shining at her. It was so bright that she turned her face away a bit – just to realize that the light continued to a specific point in the tree. It shone directly onto the center of a book, entangled in the tree’s wall. Thinking of the first book she had tried to open in the tree wall that had been drawn back deeper into the tree when she had tried to take it out she didn’t quite dare to try her luck again… but on the other hand…

She slowly reached out her hand and with slightly trembling fingers touched the book’s cover. Nothing happened at first… but then, ever so slowly the wooden beams drew away, back into the tree and after some seconds she was able to take the book out of the tree and into her hands. Curious down to the tip of her nose she opened the book.
It was empty.
You gotta be kidding me!
